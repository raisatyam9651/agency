/ 03 — Process

How we work.

A 4-phase engagement designed to find wins fast, then compound them.

/ 01

Audit & strategy

Weeks 1–3. Full technical, on-page, content, and backlink audit. Competitor and SERP analysis. We deliver a 90-day plan before we touch anything.

→ Audit report · 90-day roadmap
/ 02

Quick wins

Weeks 3–6. Indexation fixes, schema, on-page overhauls, technical blockers. We target ranking improvements you'll see in 30–60 days.

→ Indexation + on-page shipping
/ 03

Authority & content

Months 2–6. Topical content production, digital PR, link building, programmatic SEO where it fits. This is the compounding phase.

→ 4–8 pieces / mo · 8–15 links / mo
/ 04

Scale & defend

Month 6+. Defend rankings, expand to adjacent topics, build moats. Quarterly business reviews with the founder/CMO.

→ Quarterly reviews · expansion sprints

/ 06 — FAQ

Common questions.

How long until I see SEO results in India?

For competitive commercial keywords: 4–6 months. For local SEO and long-tail: 30–90 days. We set realistic expectations in the audit and report progress monthly.

Do you only do SEO, or other marketing too?

SEO is our core. We also handle content production, technical CRO, and analytics. For paid media, we partner with vetted performance agencies — happy to introduce.

Are you actually based in India?

Yes. Our SEO team is in Gurugram and Bengaluru, with clients across 12 Indian states and 4 countries. We do not outsource your work to overseas contractors.

What's the minimum contract length?

6 months. SEO compounds — shorter engagements rarely give us enough runway to prove ROI, and we'd rather be honest about that upfront than take your money and underdeliver.

Can you guarantee rankings?

No reputable agency can. Anyone who does is lying. What we guarantee: a transparent process, monthly reporting, and our full team focused on compounding your organic traffic.
